Fitness Sync

This app allows the user to login to iFit and Strava, and post their iFit activity to Strava.
While this unofficial app works with iFit and Strava, it is not created, maintained, or endorsed by iFit or Strava.
If you have any feedback or feature requests, please email hifi-remote@gxlabs.co
